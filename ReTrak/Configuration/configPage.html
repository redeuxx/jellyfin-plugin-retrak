Import from ReTrak to Jellyfin

Control how watch history from ReTrak is brought into Jellyfin.

Playback sync (Jellyfin to ReTrak)

Watching something in Jellyfin records real watch history on ReTrak via scrobbling. Manual watched/unwatched toggles in Jellyfin are not synced.

Collection and Library Sync Settings

Adds media from your Jellyfin library to your 'Collection' on ReTrak. This shows what you *have*, not what you've *watched*.
