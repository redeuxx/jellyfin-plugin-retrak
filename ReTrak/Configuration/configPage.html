Import from ReTrak to Jellyfin

Control how watch history from ReTrak is brought into Jellyfin.

Check this to prevent Jellyfin from marking an item as 'watched', even if it is marked watched on ReTrak. This protects your local watch history.
Check this to prevent Jellyfin from marking an item as 'unwatched', even if it is marked unwatched on ReTrak.
Check this to prevent Jellyfin from syncing in-progress playback status from ReTrak (e.g., "50% watched").

Sync from Jellyfin to ReTrak

Control how your Jellyfin viewing activity is sent to your ReTrak account.

Instant Sync (As it happens)

Tells ReTrak what you're watching in real-time. Recommended to be enabled for most users.
When you watch something on Jellyfin, immediately update it on ReTrak.
When you manually mark something as unwatched in Jellyfin, immediately update it on ReTrak.

Scheduled Sync (During background task)

During the periodic sync, tell ReTrak what has been watched on Jellyfin.
During the periodic sync, tell ReTrak what has been marked unwatched on Jellyfin.

Collection and Library Sync Settings

Adds media from your Jellyfin library to your 'Collection' on ReTrak. This shows what you *have*, not what you've *watched*.
Check this to *keep* items in your ReTrak collection, even after you delete the files from your Jellyfin server.

Advanced

Sends extra technical data about the file (audio/video codecs, resolution) to ReTrak when scrobbling.
For troubleshooting only. This will write detailed logs about all communication with ReTrak.
